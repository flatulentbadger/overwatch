Sean Mckenzie

In The Beginning

I was born in New Orleans, Lousiana. I grew up on a small farm in the forrested central region of the U.S. I spent the first 19 years in the same small town before moving to Boise Idaho and living on couches for several months.

Military Life

I was trained in avionics calibration and repair, shipped out to the central valley California where I spent my first 2 years on shore. I was attached to the USS Abraham Lincoln during the 2004 tsunami off the coast of Thailand and assisted relief efforts. On that same deployment I saw Hong Kong, Singapore and jumped out of planes on the north shore of Oahu.

Future Goals

I am looking for any career that contributes something to society and supports my skydiving habit.
